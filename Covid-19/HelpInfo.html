About COVID-19 (Coronavirus)

New Yorkers who have symptoms can

call 1-844-NYC-4NYC

to connect with services and schedule testing from NYC Health + Hospitals (NYC H+H).

to get important COVID-19 related updates sent straight to your phone. You can text 'COVIDESP' to get updates in Spanish.
